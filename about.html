About Us

With the continuous inflation within our nation, we aim to target some problems to reduce the economic crisis. Rodents are a factor in small businesses going into bankruptcy causing economic downfall. Alongside the economic impact, rodents can have a social impact on one’s health leading to a deteriorating health crisis. We hope our project will ease these ongoing issues while spreading awareness to residents of New York City.
